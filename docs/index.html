Molecular Architecture

Anatomy of a CAR Construct

A modular synthetic receptor with five engineered domains, each independently tunable to redirect T-cell specificity and function.

Extracellular Intracellular
T-Cell Membrane
🔬

scFv

Single-chain variable fragment (VH-linker-VL) providing MHC-independent antigen recognition. Binds surface antigens in native conformation. Optimal affinity: low nanomolar KD.

🔗

Hinge / Spacer

CD8α or IgG4-derived spacer positioning the scFv at optimal 13-15 nm immunological synapse distance. Length critically determines signaling efficiency.

🧬

Transmembrane

CD8α or CD28 domain anchoring the CAR and facilitating dimerization. CD28 TMD promotes tonic signaling; CD8α reduces activation-induced cell death.

⚡

Costimulatory

CD28: rapid expansion, glycolysis, effector phenotype via PI3K/AKT. 4-1BB: persistence, memory, mitochondrial metabolism via TRAF1/2. Provides Signal 2.

🎯

CD3ζ

Three ITAMs phosphorylated by Lck, recruiting ZAP-70 to initiate downstream TCR-like signaling cascades (PLCγ1, Ras/MAPK, NF-κB). Provides Signal 1.

Evolution

Five Generations of CARs

From the first proof-of-concept in 1989 to synthetic biology circuits in 2026.

1989 — Proof of Concept

1st Generation

Signal 1 only (CD3ζ). T cells became anergic with poor proliferation and rapid apoptosis. Failed clinically but proved redirected killing was possible.

scFv — Hinge — TMD — CD3ζ
2000s — Clinical Standard

2nd Generation

Added one costimulatory domain (CD28 or 4-1BB). 100-1000x improvement in proliferation. All 6 approved CAR-T products use this design.

scFv — Hinge — TMD — CD28 or 4-1BB — CD3ζ
Experimental

3rd Generation

Two costimulatory domains (CD28 + 4-1BB). Mixed results — excessive signaling can cause exhaustion. Domain order matters for output.

scFv — TMD — CD28 — 4-1BB — CD3ζ
TRUCKs

4th Generation

CAR + NFAT-inducible transgene (IL-12, IL-15, anti-PD-1, BiTEs). Payloads produced locally at the tumor site, avoiding systemic toxicity.

2nd-gen CAR + NFAT→IL-12 / IL-15 / anti-PD1
2020s — Synthetic Biology

5th Generation

IL-2Rβ domain with STAT3-binding YXXQ motif enables triple signaling (TCR + costim + JAK-STAT). SynNotch AND-gates, SUPRA CARs, logic circuits.

scFv — TMD — Costim — IL-2Rβ(YXXQ) — CD3ζ
Cytotoxicity

How CAR-T Cells Kill

Multiple parallel cytotoxic pathways — a single CAR-T cell can serially kill 5-20+ targets.

💀

Perforin / Granzyme Pathway

Primary mechanism. Perforin creates 16 nm pores in target membranes. Granzyme B activates caspase-3/7 and cleaves BID→tBID triggering mitochondrial apoptosis. Granzyme A activates caspase-independent death via NM23-H1 DNase. Directed degranulation at the immunological synapse.

☠

Death Receptor Pathways

Fas/FasL & TRAIL. Activated CARs upregulate FasL binding Fas(CD95) on targets → DISC formation → caspase-8 → apoptosis. TRAIL/DR4/DR5 pathway preferentially kills tumor cells. Enables bystander killing of antigen-negative cells.

🔥

Cytokine-Mediated Killing

TNF-α, IFN-γ, IL-2. Direct tumor apoptosis + TME remodeling. IFN-γ upregulates MHC-I, activates macrophages, and has anti-angiogenic effects. Recruits bystander NK cells and dendritic cells for epitope spreading.

⚔

Immunological Synapse

Non-classical synapse. Faster formation than TCR synapse (minutes vs ~30 min). Multifocal Lck clustering, MTOC reorientation, actin polarization, and directed lytic granule degranulation at the interface. Higher force generation.

Paradigm Shift

In Vivo CAR-T Generation

Eliminating ex vivo manufacturing entirely — one IV injection reprograms your own T cells.

Kelonia Therapeutics

KLN-1010 — 100% MRD-Negative

Lentiviral vector with modified VSV-G + anti-CD3 envelope. Single IV dose generates anti-BCMA CAR-T in vivo. All 4 patients MRD-negative at ASH 2025. Up to 85% of circulating T cells became CAR+. No lymphodepletion needed.

Lentiviral
MagicRNA

HN2301 — Published in NEJM

mRNA-LNP platform delivering CD19 CAR to CD8+ T cells. In 5 refractory SLE patients: 60% CD8 T cells became CAR+, complete B-cell depletion, SLEDAI scores dropped 20 points. No severe adverse events.

mRNA-LNP
Capstan / AbbVie ($2.1B Acquisition)

CPTX2309 — Targeted LNP

Anti-CD19 CAR mRNA delivered preferentially to CD8+ cytotoxic T cells via targeted LNP. Phase 1 initiated June 2025 in healthy volunteers. Transient expression enables repeat dosing without permanent modification.

Targeted LNP
Azalea Therapeutics

CRISPR-Mediated In Vivo TRAC Knock-In

Enveloped Delivery Vehicles (EDVs) deliver CRISPR-Cas9 for site-specific CAR integration at the TRAC locus in vivo. Nature March 2026: 50% CAR+ T cells from single IV dose. Permanent integration with physiologic expression.

CRISPR + EDVs
Umoja Biopharma

UB-VV111 — VivoVec Platform

Cocal-pseudotyped lentiviral vector with multi-domain fusion surface engineering. Phase 1 launched March 2025 for R/R LBCL and CLL. FDA Fast Track. Pipeline: UB-VV400 (CD22), UB-VV500 (myeloma).

Lentiviral
Interius BioTherapeutics

INT2104 — First In Vivo Trial in Europe

Targets CD7+ T and NK cells to deliver CD20 CAR transgene, creating both CAR-T and CAR-NK effectors in vivo. INVISE Phase 1 expanded to Germany (Jan 2025). Off-the-shelf, single-dose.

New Frontier

CAR-T for Autoimmune Disease

From cancer to lupus — resetting the immune system to achieve drug-free remission.

The Immune Reset Paradigm

Georg Schett's landmark NEJM series (2024) demonstrated that CD19 CAR-T cells achieve deep B-cell depletion including tissue-resident cells unreachable by anti-CD20 antibodies. Despite B-cell reconstitution at ~112 days, disease remained absent — autoantibodies did not return while protective immunity was preserved.

Kyverna's miv-cel could become the first FDA-approved CAR-T for autoimmune disease with a BLA filing planned 1H 2026 for stiff person syndrome, after showing 81% of patients exceeding clinically meaningful improvement.

Systemic Lupus (SLE)

Multiple programs: NEX-T (BMS), GC012F (AstraZeneca), BCMA-CD19 cCAR, ALLO-329 (allogeneic), HN2301 (in vivo). All showing DORIS remission and drug-free states.

💪

Myasthenia Gravis

Descartes-08 (BCMA mRNA CAR-T) advancing to Phase 3 AURORA trial — 66.7% achieved 5+ point MGC improvement vs 27.3% placebo.

🧍

Stiff Person Syndrome

Kyverna miv-cel: BLA submission 1H 2026. 26 patients, 46% median walk improvement. All endpoints significant. No high-grade CRS/ICANS.

🔬

Myositis & Scleroderma

BMS Breakfree-1: 91% achieved moderate-major improvement in IIM. Muscle strength improved 22% at 6 months. Rese-cel (Cabaletta) enrolling for SSc.

Paradigm-Shifting Breakthroughs

CRISPR discoveries, in vivo platforms, solid tumor responses, and regulatory milestones.

Nature Sep 2025

CELLFIE: CRISPR Genome-Wide Screen

Systematic CRISPR screening identified RHOG knockout as a potent CAR-T enhancer. Combined RHOG + FAS double KO showed superior efficacy across multiple in vivo models, CAR designs, and patient cells.

Nature Aug 2025

Endogenous Promoter Rewiring

CRISPR knock-in of IL-12/IL-2 under tumor-restricted endogenous promoters (NR4A2, RGS16) creates armored CAR-T that secrete cytokines only at the tumor site — zero systemic toxicity.

Nature Mar 2026

In Vivo TRAC-CAR Integration

Azalea Therapeutics: CRISPR + EDVs achieve permanent site-specific CAR integration at the TRAC locus in vivo. 50% CAR+ T cells from a single IV dose in humanized mice.

ASH Dec 2025

Anito-cel: 96% ORR in Myeloma

iMMagine-1 trial: ORR 96%, CR/sCR 74%, 95% MRD-negativity at 10⁻⁵. 24-month PFS 61.7%, OS 83%. BLA planned 2026.

Sofi-cel: First CAR-T for T-Cell Cancer

CRISPR-edited allogeneic CD7-targeting CAR-T. ORR 90.9%, CR 72.7% in T-ALL/LBL — a disease with no prior CAR-T option. FDA Breakthrough designation.

Nature BME 2026

PGE2 Receptor KO for Solid Tumors

CRISPR knockout of EP2/EP4 PGE2 receptors renders CAR-T cells resistant to TME immunosuppression. Enhanced tumor control in pancreatic, melanoma, and mesothelioma models.

Dec 2025

FDA Regulatory Framework Shift

Future CAR-T approvals in crowded indications will require randomized superiority trials vs. existing CAR-T therapies. REMS requirements eliminated for all 6 products (Jun 2025).

Jun 2025

$2.1B Validates In Vivo Platform

AbbVie acquired Capstan Therapeutics for $2.1 billion, validating the in vivo CAR-T LNP field. Capstan's CPTX2309 entered Phase 1 the same month.
